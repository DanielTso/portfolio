About Me

From the Field to the Future of Project Management

I'm not your typical construction project manager. With 33 years of experience that spans the complete project lifecycle—from hands-on trades to engineering design to construction management—I bring a perspective most professionals simply don't have. I started learning construction working summers with my father, uncle, and brothers in the family business, mastering welding, rigging, ironworking, and heavy equipment operation. After earning my BS in Construction Management, I worked as a CADD Designer and Project Designer at engineering firms specializing in infrastructure, then spent 11 years with Bechtel Corporation progressing from Field Engineer to Construction Manager. This unique journey means I understand trades work, design intent, AND field constructability—a rare combination that makes me exceptionally effective at coordinating complex projects.

My time at Bechtel—one of the world's premier engineering and construction companies—provided comprehensive exposure to large-scale project delivery. I directed superintendent teams managing 110+ personnel, coordinated with engineering and project controls on projects up to $60 million, and ensured delivery safely, on time, and within budget. What sets me apart is my continued evolution: starting from design, progressing through field and management, and now adding BIM coordination and digital project management expertise. Currently, I serve as QA BIM Coordinator for Dilling Group Inc on a mission-critical Google Datacenter project, proving I can integrate design knowledge, field expertise, and modern technical coordination seamlessly.

My career has taken me across the United States, managing diverse projects in mining, refineries, manufacturing, power plants, and now mission-critical data centers. I've stepped up in crisis moments—taking over troubled projects, managing during strikes, and delivering results when the pressure is highest. I've completed projects three weeks ahead of schedule and maintained flawless safety records on complex industrial sites.

With a BS in Construction Management from Arizona State University and continuous training in modern software platforms (ProCore, SAP, Microsoft Project, BIM), I represent the perfect combination: old-school work ethic and field expertise married with new-school technology and coordination skills.

I'm actively transitioning into project management roles where I can leverage my unique blend of extensive field experience and modern technical capabilities to deliver exceptional results for mission-critical and heavy industrial projects.
